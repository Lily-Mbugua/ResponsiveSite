The Saccharine Flower: (Pronunced sacc-aah-rine) is a labor of love from me. The name is simply a humorous play on my name.



'Saccharine' means sweet, which i like to think I am and 'Flower' is just a lame attempt at making my name sound better but to answer your question my name is Lily. Join me on this adventure while we travel off the beaten track while creating memories that will last forever.



Through this blog I hope you join me in discovering all things tech while simultaneously understanding that travel is an adventure, discovery, culture and surprise. A S.F (Saccharine Flower) moment, indeed. And the same could be said for my love of cooking and dining, for cultivating friendships that inspire you, for the idea of “beauty” and what that means to us.



An understanding that comes with living your life to the fullest so that it bursts at the seams. An unbridled joy. An insatiable curiosity. That’s what The Saccharine Flower means to me. Thank you for joining me on this adventure! I could not be more grateful for your support and companionship on this beautifully wild ride.



Last but not least, Never forget: 'Always we Begin Again'
